Carpet Installation Guide

Carpet costs include the material, padding, and installation labor. Average residential carpet installation costs $3-12 per square foot all-in.

Carpet Quality Levels:

  • Budget: $2-4/sq ft. Basic quality, shorter lifespan (3-5 years)
  • Standard: $4-8/sq ft. Good quality, average lifespan (7-10 years)
  • Premium: $8-15/sq ft. High quality, long lifespan (10-15 years)
  • Luxury: $15+/sq ft. Exceptional quality, extended durability (15+ years)

Padding Selection:

  • Standard: 5/16-3/8 inch, adequate for most homes
  • Upgraded: 7/16-1/2 inch, better comfort and durability
  • Premium: Memory foam or specialty padding, maximum comfort

Carpet Pile Types:

  • Saxony: Most popular, plush feel, moderate wear resistance
  • Frieze: Twisted fibers, excellent durability, hides footprints
  • Berber: Looped pile, very durable, good for high-traffic areas
  • Shag: Long, luxurious, requires more maintenance

Installation Factors:

Installation costs depend on room complexity, seaming needs, and stairs. Complex layouts with many seams cost more. Removal of old carpet typically adds $1-2/sq ft.

Carpet Care & Longevity:

  • Professional cleaning every 12-18 months extends life
  • Vacuuming 2-3 times weekly prevents wear
  • Use area rugs in high-traffic zones
  • Address stains immediately for best results

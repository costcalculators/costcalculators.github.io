Paint Coverage Guide

Most interior paints cover 250-400 square feet per gallon depending on surface texture and number of coats. This calculator adjusts for surface type automatically.

Coverage by Surface:

  • Smooth Walls: 350-400 sq ft per gallon
  • Textured Drywall: 300-350 sq ft per gallon
  • Popcorn Ceiling: 200-250 sq ft per gallon
  • Exterior Rough: 200-300 sq ft per gallon

Number of Coats:

1 Coat: Good for touch-ups and repaints of same color. 2 Coats: Standard for fresh colors and new surfaces. 3+ Coats: For dramatic color changes or poor coverage.

Paint Types & Costs:

  • Budget Paint: $15-25/gallon (thin, more coats needed)
  • Standard Paint: $25-40/gallon (good quality, recommended)
  • Premium Paint: $40-70/gallon (excellent durability, covers better)
  • Specialty Paint: $50-100/gallon (mildew-resistant, textured, etc)

Professional Painting Labor:

Professional painters typically charge $2-6 per square foot or $50-150 per hour. Prices vary by region, project complexity, and painter experience.

Money-Saving Tips:

  • Buy paint in bulk for discounts
  • DIY when possible to save 50%+ on labor
  • Prime before painting for better coverage
  • Paint in moderate temperatures for best results

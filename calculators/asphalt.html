Asphalt Driveway Costs & Maintenance

Asphalt is a popular, cost-effective driveway material. New installation typically costs $3-6 per square foot, while resurfacing costs $1.50-3 per square foot.

Project Types:

  • New Installation: Base preparation plus 3-4 inches asphalt. Cost: $3-6/sq ft
  • Resurface/Overlay: Add 2-3 inch layer over existing. Cost: $1.50-3/sq ft
  • Patch Repairs: Fill holes and cracks. Cost: $10-20 per patch
  • Seal Coating: Protective sealant. Cost: $0.15-0.30/sq ft

Asphalt Thickness:

  • 2 inches: Adequate for overlay over existing base
  • 3 inches: Standard residential driveway
  • 4 inches: Heavy-duty, commercial, truck traffic

Asphalt Maintenance Schedule:

  • Seal Coating: Every 2-3 years ($0.15-0.30/sq ft)
  • Crack Filling: Annually, as needed
  • Patching: When holes appear
  • Resurfacing: Every 15-20 years
  • Full Replacement: Every 25-30 years

Regional Cost Variations:

Asphalt costs vary by region and season. Northern climates may cost 10-20% more due to weather demands. Summer is peak season with potential premium pricing.

Extend Asphalt Life:

Proper maintenance significantly extends driveway life. Seal coating every 2-3 years prevents water damage and adds 5-10 years to driveway life.

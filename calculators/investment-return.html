Understanding Investment Returns

This calculator demonstrates the power of compound interest over time. By regularly contributing to your investments and earning returns, your money can grow significantly.

How It Works

The calculator uses the compound interest formula to project your investment growth. Each year, you earn returns not just on your initial investment, but also on all previous earnings.

Investment Tips

  • Start Early: Time is your greatest asset in investing
  • Consistent Contributions: Regular investments can build substantial wealth
  • Diversify: Spread investments across different asset classes
  • Reinvest Earnings: Maximize compound interest effects
  • Long-term Focus: Weather market volatility by staying invested
  • Risk Tolerance: Adjust returns based on your risk profile

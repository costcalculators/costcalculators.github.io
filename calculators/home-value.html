How Home Values Are Estimated

Professional appraisers use multiple methods to estimate home values. This calculator uses the market comparison approach, the most common method for residential properties.

Valuation Factors:

  • Comparable Sales: Recent sales of similar properties nearby
  • Square Footage: Size adjustments based on price per square foot
  • Property Condition: Repairs needed or exceptional updates
  • Market Trends: Local appreciation or depreciation
  • Features: Bedrooms, bathrooms, and special amenities

Comparable Sales (Comps):

The most reliable approach uses recent sales of similar homes in your area. Look for properties that sold within the last 3-6 months and are similar in size, condition, and location.

Market Appreciation:

Real estate markets appreciate at different rates depending on location. National average is 3-4% annually, but can vary from -5% to +10% depending on local economic conditions.

When to Get a Professional Appraisal:

This calculator provides an estimate. For refinancing, mortgage applications, estate planning, or major financial decisions, hire a licensed professional appraiser.

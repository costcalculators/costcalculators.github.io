Concrete Project Planning

Concrete is measured in cubic yards. One cubic yard covers 81 square feet at 4 inches thick. This calculator automatically converts your measurements.

Standard Thickness Guidelines:

  • Patios & Walkways: 4 inches (minimum 3-4 inches)
  • Driveways: 4-6 inches (heavier use requires more)
  • Foundations: 8-12 inches depending on soil and loads
  • Pool Decks: 4 inches minimum, often thicker for durability

Concrete Strength (PSI):

  • 3000 PSI: Light-duty, residential, patios
  • 4000 PSI: Standard driveways, most residential use
  • 5000+ PSI: Heavy-duty, commercial, high traffic

Cost Factors:

Concrete costs average $120-200 per cubic yard, varying by region and type. Decorative concrete (stamped, colored) costs significantly more. Labor typically adds $2-8 per square foot depending on complexity.

Concrete Care & Maintenance:

  • Cure for 7 days before heavy use
  • Seal every 2-3 years for longevity
  • Avoid salt and harsh chemicals
  • Fill cracks promptly to prevent spreading

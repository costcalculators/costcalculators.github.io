Debt Payoff Strategies

Getting out of debt requires a solid strategy. The most important factor is consistencyâ€”make regular payments and avoid adding new debt while paying off existing balances.

Popular Debt Payoff Methods

  • Debt Snowball: Pay smallest debts first for psychological wins, then move to larger debts
  • Debt Avalanche: Pay highest interest debts first to save money on interest
  • Debt Consolidation: Combine multiple debts into one lower-interest loan
  • Balance Transfer: Move high-interest credit card debt to lower-interest card
  • Negotiation: Ask creditors to lower interest rates or provide hardship programs

Debt Reduction Tips

  • Create a detailed budget to identify extra funds for debt payoff
  • Stop using credit while paying off existing debt
  • Consider side income to accelerate payoff
  • Make extra payments when possible to reduce interest
  • Contact creditors if experiencing hardship
  • Monitor your credit score and credit report

Prevention

  • Build an emergency fund to avoid credit card debt
  • Use credit cards wisely and pay in full each month
  • Understand interest rates and fees before borrowing
  • Maintain good financial habits

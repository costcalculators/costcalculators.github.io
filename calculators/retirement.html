Retirement Planning Essentials

Proper retirement planning ensures you have sufficient funds to maintain your lifestyle after you stop working. This calculator helps estimate the total amount needed.

Key Retirement Planning Concepts

  • Safe Withdrawal Rate: Typically 3-4% annually from your retirement portfolio
  • Inflation Impact: Costs increase over time, reducing purchasing power
  • Life Expectancy: Plan for a long retirement (20-30+ years)
  • Healthcare Costs: Often the largest retirement expense
  • Social Security: Available at 62-70 years old, provides baseline income

Retirement Savings Vehicles

  • 401(k): Employer-sponsored plan with matching contributions
  • IRA (Traditional): Tax-deferred individual account
  • Roth IRA: Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
  • Pension: Guaranteed income from previous employers
  • Taxable Accounts: Additional savings beyond retirement accounts

Action Steps

  • Start saving as early as possible to maximize compound growth
  • Contribute to employer 401(k) to get matching funds
  • Diversify investments based on your risk tolerance
  • Review and rebalance portfolio annually
  • Consult a financial advisor for personalized planning

Construction Cost Estimation

Construction costs vary widely based on project type, location, quality standards, and market conditions. Use this calculator to get a rough estimate for budgeting purposes.

Cost Per Square Foot Guidelines:

  • Residential: $100-300+ per sq ft depending on quality
  • Commercial: $150-400+ per sq ft
  • Industrial: $75-250+ per sq ft
  • Renovation: $150-400+ per sq ft

Regional Variations:

Labor and materials costs vary significantly by region. Coastal areas and major cities typically cost 20-40% more than rural areas. Cost inflation also affects different regions differently.

Quality Levels Impact:

Budget construction uses basic materials and finishes. Premium includes higher-quality materials and finishes. Luxury includes high-end finishes, custom work, and premium materials throughout.

What's Included in Soft Costs:

  • Architectural and engineering design fees
  • Permits and inspections
  • Project management
  • Insurance and bonding
  • Legal and surveying fees

Contingency Reserve:

A contingency reserve (typically 10-20%) accounts for unexpected costs, changes, and price fluctuations. Always budget for contingencies in construction projects.
